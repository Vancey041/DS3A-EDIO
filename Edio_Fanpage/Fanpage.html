TEAM 7

Naruto is a spirited and determined ninja who dreams of becoming Hokage despite being shunned for housing the Nine-Tails within him.

His optimism, perseverance, and belief in bonds inspire those around him.

Sasuke is a talented but brooding ninja driven by vengeance against his brother for destroying their clan. His cold determination contrasts with his deep, unspoken bond with his teammates.

Kakashi, the mysterious and laid-back leader of Team 7, hides incredible skill and wisdom beneath his mask. He values teamwork above all, shaped by the painful lessons of his past.

Sakura starts as a book-smart but insecure kunoichi, gradually growing into a strong and capable medic-nin. Her loyalty and compassion balance her fiery temper and determination.
