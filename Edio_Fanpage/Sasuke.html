Sasuke Uchiha

  • Full Name: Sasuke Uchiha
  • Age: 12 (Part I), 16–17 (Part II)
  • Rank: Genin
  • Clan: Uchiha Clan
  • Affiliation: Konohagakure (formerly), Rogue Ninja
  • Team: Team 7
  • Birthday: July 23

Sasuke Uchiha is a prodigy of the Uchiha Clan and Naruto’s rival. His life is shaped by tragedy when his older brother, Itachi, annihilates their clan. Consumed by vengeance, Sasuke dedicates himself to growing stronger, often isolating himself from those who care for him.

Though his path leads him into darkness and away from his comrades, his deep connection to Naruto and Team 7 never truly fades. Sasuke embodies the struggle between revenge and redemption, making his journey one of the most complex and emotional in the shinobi world.
