Kakashi Hatake

  • Full Name: Kakashi Hatake
  • Age: 26–27 (Part I), 29–31 (Part II)
  • Rank: Jonin
  • Affiliation: Konohagakure (Hidden Leaf Village)
  • Team: Leader of Team 7
  • Birthday: September 15

Kakashi Hatake, known as the "Copy Ninja," is the calm and enigmatic leader of Team 7. Despite his laid-back demeanor, he is one of the most skilled shinobi of his generation, mastering a thousand jutsu with his Sharingan.

His past is marked by tragedy — losing his father, teammates, and sensei. These experiences shape his philosophy that teamwork is more important than individual talent. Beneath his mask and aloofness lies a man deeply loyal to his comrades, guiding Naruto, Sasuke, and Sakura with patience and wisdom.
