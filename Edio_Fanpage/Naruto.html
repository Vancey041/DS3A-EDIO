Naruto Uzumaki

  • Full Name: Naruto Uzumaki
  • Age: 12 (Part I), 16 (Part II)
  • Rank: Genin
  • Affiliation: Konohagakure (Hidden Leaf Village)
  • Team: Team 7 (with Sasuke, Sakura, Kakashi)
  • Birthday: October 10

Naruto Uzumaki is a spirited ninja who dreams of becoming Hokage, the leader of his village, so that everyone will recognize his existence. Orphaned at birth and shunned by villagers because he carries the Nine-Tails Fox spirit within him, Naruto endured loneliness and pain. Despite this, he grew up with an unshakable optimism, finding strength in his friendships and his unyielding determination to never give up.

Over time, Naruto's willpower, kindness, and belief in bonds inspire his comrades and even change the hearts of his enemies. His journey is one of resilience, growth, and an unwavering pursuit of peace and understanding in a world torn by conflict.
